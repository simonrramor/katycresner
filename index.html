What we do

The first thing we do when helping you plan your celebration is get to know you. It’s vitally important that we understand your personality, your likes and dislikes, your style and taste. We listen closely to what you have in mind. It’s your moment, and if we have one aim, it’s to exceed your expectations, making it an occasion that will live long in the memory of everyone who was there.

Designing the perfect day.

First steps

An initial chat allows us to get to know you, gain a full understanding of your requirements and tell you about the many ways in which we can help. This initial consultation is, of course, free of charge and you are under absolutely no obligation to make further use of our services. Our primary objective is to alleviate the stress associated with planning and holding an event.

How can we help?

With years of experience behind us and and great working relationships with a host of leading caterers, entertainers, producers, florists, photographers, videographers and venues, we offer the most comprehensive service available. From initial planning to the big day itself, we can arrange and oversee every detail, doing our utmost to ensure that everything runs seamlessly and leaving you free to enjoy yourself.

On the day.

This is where all those months of planning come together. We’re there to ensure that every last detail is taken care of, from arranging easy access for suppliers to straightening that buttonhole or arranging last minute pressing of a creased shirt. Our reputation, of which we’re extremely proud, stands or falls on our ability to help you to enjoy an absolutely extraordinary day.
